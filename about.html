About Zephyr

Credit scores as a metric is always confined within country borders. Hundreds of millions immigrate to different countries in search of better educational and professional opportunities and most of them have an american equivalent prime score.

But everybody are forced to start from 0 and struggle to get even basic financial products, lease or even a postpaid mobile connection. Immigrants continue to make up significant chunk of population in North America, Middle East and the list continues to grow every year.

We are here building the future of credit data infrastructure. We enable institutions to assess the creditworthiness of immigrants using their international credit history and transaction data. We are building the future where everybody no matter where they are from has fair and equal access to quality finance. We are on a mission to make credit scores truly universal. Changing million lives one step at a time.
